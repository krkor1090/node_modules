Class: CollectionGroup

CollectionGroup

new CollectionGroup()

Creates a new collection group. Collection groups allow single operations to be propagated to multiple collections at once. CRUD operations against a collection group are in fed to the group's collections. Useful when separating out slightly different data into multiple collections but querying as one collection.
